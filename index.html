Block-For-Ex



Bringing Blockchain on Forex.

A new concept of bringing the Forex Market on to the Blockchain.

Get Started

Problem

Within forex, one would trade a currency pair in the form of a derivative such as Futures and Options or directly the pair itself. When the pair is directly traded, one sells a currency in exchange for another one.
Banks/institutions have the resources to "control" the volume of the underlying asset which often leads to technical events such as stop-loss hunting.

Need

A forex market is a decentralized regulatory, whereas the brokersâ€™ OMS- order management system is centralized.Hereby, this leads to delay in process of buying and selling, and even volume manipulation.

Solution

What we intend to do is take the entire Order Management System onto a blockchain platform, thereby making it decentralised. This will allow a buyer to directly perform transactions with a seller at a within a price band set by a regulatory body. Traders will be able to directly trade the asset/instrument/currency pairs directly with another trader.
